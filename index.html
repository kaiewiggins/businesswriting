Business Writing Solutions

Content and strategic services for American and Canadian businesses.


           



BWS provides content and technical writing, copy editing, proofreading, planning and marketing solutions
for English-speaking corporations, small-businesses and non-profit organizations.

The right solution always starts with a question:

Why?

Not what. Not how. Why.

Why get a new website? Why rework your copy? Why market yourself online? Why change your systems?

Why gives the answer to what. And how.

Business Writing Solutions helps you ask and understand your why better, so we can create the right what using a range of how:

  • copy-editing and proofreading
  • technical writing: reports, proposals, marketing & sales content, internal & external communications
  • content direction & copywriting
  • systems, database or template development
  • website creation or redesign
  • business systems consulting
  • research and data visualization
  • business, marketing or promotion plans
  • employee training or coaching

We've been providing in-house and remote strategic marketing, business and writing services to the Vancouver market, across Canada and the United States, as well as abroad, from the beautiful Pacific Northwest for over a decade.
